THE STORY
BEHIND THE SAUCE

HELLO, I’M LAUREN AND AS A LIFELONG
ADVENTUROUS EATER, I’VE TRIED A LOT OF
CRAZY THINGS, BUT I NEVER THOUGHT HOT
SAUCE IN COFFEE WAS A
SANE OPTION UNTIL A FRIEND DARED ME TO TRY IT.

AND IT SUCKED.

BUT I WONDERED IF THERE WAS A WAY TO ADD
SPICE TO MY COFFEE. WHEN THERE WAS NO
EASY SOLUTION, UJJO WAS BORN.

UJJO (OOO-JOE): THE WORD FOR “FIRE” IN
KONKANI, SPOKEN ON THE WEST COAST OF
INDIA, IS AN HOMAGE AND A LOVE LETTER TO
MY DAD, AN INDIAN IMMIGRANT WHO
INSTILLED A FIRE IN MY
PALATE – AND MY HEART – FROM THE VERY BEGINNING.
